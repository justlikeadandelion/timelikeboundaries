The workshop aims to bring together a select group of experts in the topic of timelike boundaries in gravity, spanning various fields from mathematical relativity to quantum gravity and holography. The one-week event will provide a platform for in-depth discussions and collaboration among participants.

The meeting will take place in the beautiful UNESCO’s world heritage city of Morelia, Mexico, hosted by the Universidad Michoacana de San Nicolás de Hidalgo, the place where one of the first review articles on the subject was written.
